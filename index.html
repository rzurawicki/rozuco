About

I am a Junior at Harvard, involved in HackHarvard and the Harvard/MIT Educational Studies Program. Currently, I research proof-checking software and programming languages. I also contribute to packages of the Meteor project and teach Meteor to other students.

I research how computer science research can make programming easier and more accessible. My personal and academic interests include education, mobile development, distributed systems, foundational mathematics, linguistics, philosophy, and music.

reSound

reSound is a brand new way to listen to music. Your music will follow you wherever you go, without skipping a beat.

reSound uses the latest advancements in Bluetooth 4.0 LE to bring you a responsive and dynamic listening experience among all your connected devices. Never miss a beat with reSound superior response time. Music will relay between your devices in fractions of a seconds, unnoticeable to the human ear.
